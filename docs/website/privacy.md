# Privacy Policy

Immich Lounge is designed to work with your own immich server and Roku devices.

## What We Collect

The project itself does not provide a hosted cloud service and does not intentionally collect personal data from users.

Depending on how you run it:

- the companion app stores your local configuration
- the Roku app stores limited local settings and cached slideshow data
- your immich server remains the source of your media and metadata

## Third-Party Services

The companion and Roku apps may interact with third-party services only when you enable related features, such as weather data providers or GitHub Pages for documentation hosting.

## Your Data

Photos, metadata, and API access remain under your control through your immich deployment and local network setup.
